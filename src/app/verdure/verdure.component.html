VERDURE

I have a bit of a sweet tooth. Keeping healthy is a bit of a struggle for myself and certain others in my family. Also ensuring the right balance of vitamins and minerals can also be difficult for someone who works full time and cannot easily control every aspect of their diet.

Not to mention the stress of emigrating and getting married. Its very easy to eat away stress when things in life are out of your control.

My Verdure App was designed for me and my family to help track meals and snacks for calories to help get an idea of how to avoid over eating or eating badly. Calorie tracking isn't the be all and end all in weight loss and health, but it plays a part.

The App is written in C# .Net and Xamarin with support from .Net Core Libraries and Unit Tested using SpecFlow. This gives it cross-device support which is something I'm keen on integrating into all my projects where possible, and when the funds are available I may extend this with an online web portal and multi-device support so data isn't tied to a single device.

For data storage it currently uses Sqlite, but I may change this to Realm, but data security is something I'd want to really put in place before considering commercialising the App. Also I'm not sure if it would fall under HIPPA Regulations or other health regulations so I'd need to research that before releasing to the public. For the time being this is just for me and the family.

The basic App tracks Meals and Snacks, along with their calorie intakes across a day. More advanced versions are planned which track exercise to take it into account when calculating calorie expenditure, some simplified data analysis to help track the most effective balance of calories and exercise for an individual so they can get the most out of their App, and possibly an inclusion of Vitamin and Mineral tracking to ensure the user is eating a balanced diet and isn't deficient in any major elements of diet.
